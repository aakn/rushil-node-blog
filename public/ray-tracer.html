Ray Tracer

Individual Project

Software/Tools:

Description:
Ray tracer done as a part of VIZA 656 Image Synthesis in the spring of 2015. Multiple projects were done and some of the results are presented here.
This ray tracer has many features:
---- Positionable cameras
---- Lambert, Gooch, and Phong shading
---- Point, Directional, and Spot Lights
---- Surface textures, procedural checker patterns, 2D Perlin noise
---- Triangle mesh generation from OBJ files
---- Reflection and Refraction
---- Environment maps, Normal maps
---- Depth of field (bokeh)

Results:
Phong shading with (L) one light source and (R) multiple light sources.
