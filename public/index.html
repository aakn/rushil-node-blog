Background

I'm a Graphics Software Engineer currently working at HERE Technologies. I graduated with a Master's in Visualization from Texas A&M University in December 2017. In the past, I have interned at SideFX Software and Framestore on their FX simulation teams.

I have been interested in computer graphics since I was 13 years old. My main areas of interest are rendering and simulation, and I'm keen to see how Machine Learning and AI play a role in taking computer graphics further.
