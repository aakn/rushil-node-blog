Hello,
I'm Rushil

and I love computer graphics

I'm a graduate student at Texas A&M University in the Department of Visualization a.k.a The Viz Lab. I am currently pursuing my Masters degree. I hold a Bachelors degree in Computer Science & Engineering and have always had a keen interest in Computer Graphics.

My main area of interest is Effects. I enjoy working with Houdini, even though I'm still learning. I find it highly frustrating, extremely rewarding, and also a bit addictive. My programming language of choice is C++. I love working on environmental effects since the vast number of natural phenomena in our world gives me the opportunity to simulate something different each time I pick up a new project to work on.

I also find work related to proceduralism to be very interesting. Real time FX is another area I find myself exploring when I get the time to. I have recently begun reading into rendering topics a bit more deeply as well. I consider web and graphic design to be a hobby.

I love football (soccer) and support Liverpool FC. They've been a mainstay in my life since 2005. In 2015, I finally got to visit the city of Liverpool and also take a tour of Anfield, Liverpool's home stadium. It was one of the most memorable experiences of my life.

My time here at the Viz Lab has been wonderful so far. I've had the pleasure of meeting and hanging out with some truly gifted individuals here and I'm enjoying each and every minute of it.
