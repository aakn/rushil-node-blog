Flocking Simulation

Individual project

Software/Tools:

Description:
One of the projects for VIZA 659 (Physically Based Modeling). This simulation follows Craig Reynold's paper on flocking from SIGGRAPH '97.
All the main features of flocking have been implemented:
Flock centering
Collision avoidance
Velocity matching
