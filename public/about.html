I'm a FX Technical Director, and I'm currently a graduate student studying at Texas A&M University in the Department of Visualization a.k.a The Viz Lab. I hold a Bachelors degree in Computer Science & Engineering and have always had a keen interest in Computer Graphics.

My main area of interest is Effects. I enjoy working with Houdini, even though I'm still learning. I find it highly frustrating, extremely rewarding, and also a bit addictive. My programming language of choice is C++. I love working on environmental effects since the vast number of natural phenomena in our world gives me the opportunity to simulate something different each time I pick up a new project to work on.

I also find work related to proceduralism to be very interesting. I have recently begun reading into rendering topics a bit more deeply as well. I consider web and graphic design to be a hobby.

I also like football (soccer) and support Liverpool FC.

My time here at the Viz Lab has been wonderful so far. I've had the pleasure of meeting and hanging out with some truly gifted individuals here and I'm enjoying each and every minute of it.
