KNOT TODAY

Team Members: Alyssa Pena, Bill Derrington, Emily Oswald, Qiao Wang, Yuxiao Du, and me

My Role: FX & Art Lead

This was a 30 second animated short produced in the Summer of 2015 under the guidance and mentorship of artists and CG professionals from Walt Disney Animation Studios as part of an ongoing collaboration with the Department of Visualization.

While primarily the FX lead, I helped out with modeling and texturing of few props in the early stages of production.

FLOCKING SIMULATION

Individual project

Flocking simulation done in Fall 2015 as a part of Physically Based Modeling (VIZA659)

The project is based on Craig Reynolds paper Flocks, Herds, and Schools: A Distributed Behavioral Model. It follows the three basic steering behaviors as explained by Reynolds.
